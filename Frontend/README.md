# Project Title
Online Football Products Store Management System

## Project Proposal Overview
This project aims to design and analyze an online web-based system that allows users to browse, search, and purchase football-related products such as jerseys, football boots, balls, accessories, and training equipment.
The system will provide a secure, user-friendly shopping experience for customers and an efficient management panel for administrators.

## Main Goals of the System
1-Provide an online platform for selling football products.
2-Allow customers to easily browse, search, and purchase products.
3-Enable administrators to manage products, orders, and users efficiently.
4-Improve customer experience through fast access, secure payments, and order tracking.
5-Reduce manual store operations and increase business reach.

## Problem Solved
Traditional football stores depend heavily on physical presence, which limits:
1-Customer reach
2-Operating hours
3-Inventory management efficiency
The proposed system solves these problems by:
1-Allowing 24/7 online shopping
2-Automating order processing and inventory updates
3-Making football products accessible to a larger audience

## Main Uses of the System
1-Browsing football products by category
2-Searching for products by name, brand, or price
3-Viewing product details
4-Registering and logging in
5-Adding products to cart
6-Placing and tracking orders
7-Managing products and orders (Admin)

## Conclusion
The Football Products Store Management System provides a complete solution for selling football products online. It enhances customer convenience, improves business efficiency, and supports future expansion. The system follows standard system analysis and design principles to ensure quality, security, and usability.
